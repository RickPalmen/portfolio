Animo

I created a concept for Cubiss, an organization focused on promoting reading development among children and teenagers. I designed an app that gives teens a glimpse into the favorite books of their idols—artists, athletes, social media stars — anyone can share the books that helped shape their journey.

Coding Safari

In Code Safari, we set out to introduce young people to the world of coding and show them that it can be a powerful and creative outlet—not just a technical skill. We visited a high school where we held interactive workshops, guiding students through the process of using code to create digital art. By combining technology with creativity, we aimed to make coding more approachable, fun, and inspiring for a new generation.

Heins Multimedia Story

In this project, we worked with datasets provided by CBS, a major organization that collects and analyzes data in the Netherlands. They challenged us to create a multimedia story that would offer an immersive experience for the reader and make the data more engaging. We explored beer exports from the Netherlands and Belgium, taking a deep dive into how the two countries differ.
