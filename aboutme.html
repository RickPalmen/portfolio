About
Me

Rick Palmen

Hey there, I’m a 22-year-old designer and student navigating the ever-evolving world of visual communication. I’ve got a love for clean, slick aesthetics—minimal but purposeful design that quietly makes an impact. I’m drawn to details in style, whether it’s in graphic design, fashion, or the moody, darker tones I naturally lean toward. I’m a calm and introverted person who likes to take my time to observe, think, and create. Lately, I’ve been getting into photography—learning to capture the same moods and moments I aim for in my design work.

When I’m not designing or studying, I’m probably online. Chronically. Whether I’m saving inspiration, laughing at dumb memes, or deep-diving into a new creative interest, the internet’s a big part of how I learn and stay inspired. That said, I also love getting out into the real world—especially big cities. The energy, culture, and constant movement fuel me in ways nothing else does. I’ve dabbled in coding just enough to understand the basics, but my heart’s in the visual side of things—making stuff that looks good, feels right, and functions well. I’m always trying to grow and push my design skills further.

A lot of my drive comes from my dad. He’s always told me to do what I love and work hard for it. I carry that with me. I’m not trying to be anything I’m not—just someone who stays true to their own pace and path. To most people, I probably seem quiet and laid-back—and that’s not wrong. But if you know me, you’ll know there’s also a funny, chaotic, slightly dumb side in there too. It’s all part of the package.

RICK PALMEN
